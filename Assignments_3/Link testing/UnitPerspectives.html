This page will be a helpfull resource where students can get a better understanding of the many views on major events in U.S. History. To use this page find an event you are curious of and then select the perspective you wish to follow. After ward you will be sent to a page where several links to trusted resources will give you information partaking to the event you chose.
Event

Here will be a brief descripton of the event that correlates to the unit the class is currently in. Then the links will be diffent perspective that the student can view the events trough. For example during the "Trail of Tears" a student can view the event trough the eyes of the Native Americans or the US settlers or other parties
